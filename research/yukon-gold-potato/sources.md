# Yukon Gold Potato - Research Sources

## Key Findings

**Variety:** Mid-early season yellow-fleshed potato
**Developed:** Canada, 1980s
**DTM:** 90 days (range 80-100)
**Uses:** All-purpose - boiling, mashing, roasting

---

## Days to Maturity: 90 (HIGH)

**Range:** 80-100 days (mid-early season)
- [Territorial Seed](https://territorialseed.com/products/yukon-gold) - 80-95 days
- [Home for Harvest](https://homefortheharvest.com/yukon-gold-potatoes/) - ~100 days
- [SFGATE](https://homeguides.sfgate.com/times-plant-harvest-yukon-gold-potatoes-22666.html) - 90 days
- [Urban Farmer](https://www.ufseeds.com/product/yukon-gold-seed-potatoes/POYU.html) - 65 days (early "new" potatoes)

**Used 90 days** - median for full-size maturity

---

## Yield: 1.5 lbs/plant (MEDIUM)

- [SFGATE](https://homeguides.sfgate.com/times-plant-harvest-yukon-gold-potatoes-22666.html) - "about 1 pound" per plant, 100 lbs per 100 ft row
- Sources note "heavy yields" but lower than late-season varieties
- **Used 1.5 lbs** - between early varieties (1 lb) and late varieties (2 lbs)

---

## Spacing: 0.4 plants/sq ft (HIGH)

- [Johnny's Seeds](https://www.johnnyseeds.com/vegetables/potatoes/yukon-gold-seed-potatoes-532.html) - Large upright plants
- [Urban Farmer](https://www.ufseeds.com/product/yukon-gold-seed-potatoes/POYU.html) - 8-12" plant spacing
- [Home for Harvest](https://homefortheharvest.com/yukon-gold-potatoes/) - 10-12" × 30-36"
- Calculation: 10" × 30" = 300 sq in = 2.08 sq ft = **0.48 plants/sq ft**
- **Used 0.4** (slightly wider, conservative)

---

## Seed Cost: $0.45/plant (HIGH)

- [Urban Farmer](https://www.ufseeds.com/product/yukon-gold-seed-potatoes/POYU.html) - $2.75/lb (20 lb), $7.50/lb (2 lb)
- [Johnny's Seeds](https://www.johnnyseeds.com/vegetables/potatoes/yukon-gold-seed-potatoes-532.html) - $1.30/lb (25 lb), $5.50/lb (5 lb)
- 1 lb = ~5 seed potatoes
- Mid-range: $2.25/lb ÷ 5 = **$0.45/plant**
