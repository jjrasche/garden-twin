# Red Norland Potato - Research Sources

## Key Findings

**Variety:** Early-season red-skinned potato
**DTM:** 70-90 days (median 80)
**Uses:** Boiling, roasting, fresh eating (not ideal for long storage)

---

- [Veseys](https://www.veseys.com/ca/norland-potatoes-19970k.html) - 70-90 days

## Yield: 2 lbs/plant (MEDIUM)

## Spacing: 0.4 plants/sq ft (HIGH)

- [High Mowing](https://www.highmowingseeds.com/organic-non-gmo-dark-red-norland-potato.html) - 12" plant × 36" row
- [Home for the Harvest](https://homefortheharvest.com/norland-potatoes/) - 10-12" × 24-36"
- Calculation: 10" × 30" = 300 sq in = 2.08 sq ft = **0.48 plants/sq ft**
- Used 0.4 (slightly wider, conservative)

## Seed Cost: $0.40/plant (HIGH)

- $2.00/lb ÷ 5 = **$0.40/plant**
