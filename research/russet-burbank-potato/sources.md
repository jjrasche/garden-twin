# Russet Burbank Potato - Research Sources (Streamlined)

## Key Findings

**Variety:** Late-season storage potato, 110-120 days to maturity
**Developed:** Luther Burbank, 1902
**Uses:** Baking, frying, mashing (industry standard)

---

## Days to Maturity: 110 (HIGH confidence)

**Range:** 95-120 days across sources, median ~110
- [Filaree Organic](https://filareefarm.com/russet-burbank-potato/) - 100-120 days
- [Arts Nursery](https://www.artsnursery.com/product/urban-balconist-seed-potatoes-russet-burbank-64322) - 110-120 days
- [U of Arkansas Extension](https://www.uaex.uada.edu/yard-garden/resource-library/plant-week/burbank-potato.aspx) - 110 days
- [Gardening Know How](https://www.gardeningknowhow.com/edible/vegetables/potato/types-of-potatoes.htm) - 95 days (early harvest)

**Used 110 days** - late-season storage potato

---

## Yield: 2 lbs/plant (MEDIUM-HIGH confidence)

- [CSU Extension via SFGATE](https://homeguides.sfgate.com/average-potato-yield-per-plant-48132.html) - ~2 lbs per plant (good conditions)
- General rule: 10 lbs harvest per 1 lb seed planted
- Experienced gardeners: 4-5 lbs/plant = "excellent"
- Russet Burbank noted as "hefty above average yield"

**Used 2 lbs** - conservative estimate for average garden conditions

---

## Spacing: 0.33 plants/sq ft (HIGH confidence)

- [High Mowing](https://www.highmowingseeds.com/organic-non-gmo-burbank-russet-potato.html) - 12" plant × 30-36" row
- [Luv2Garden](https://luv2garden.com/grow-russet-burbank-potatoes/) - 12-14" × 36"
- Calculation: 12" × 36" = 432 sq in = 3 sq ft/plant = **0.33 plants/sq ft**

---

## Seed Cost: $0.60/plant (HIGH confidence)

**Pricing:**
- [High Mowing](https://www.highmowingseeds.com/organic-non-gmo-burbank-russet-potato.html) - $3.68/lb (20 lb qty), $7.52/lb (2.5 lb)
- [Urban Farmer](https://www.ufseeds.com/product/russet-burbank-seed-potatoes/PORB.html) - $3.50/lb (10 lb), $7.50/lb (2 lb)

**Calculation:**
- 1 lb seed = ~6 seed potatoes (or cut pieces)
- $3.68/lb ÷ 6 plants/lb = **$0.61 ≈ $0.60/plant**
