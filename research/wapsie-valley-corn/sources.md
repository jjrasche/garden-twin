# Wapsie Valley Corn - Research Sources

## Variety Background

**HIGH CONFIDENCE**

Wapsie Valley Dent was developed by Adolph Steinbronn of Bremer County, Iowa, from a cross between Reid's Yellow Dent and Golden Glow, and released to growers in 1971. Original producer: Artesian Organic Farm (Steinbronn family farm).

**Sources:**
- [Thresh Seed Co](https://www.threshseed.com/products/wapsie-valley-dent-corn)
- [Tomorrow Seeds](https://tomorrowseeds.com/products/wapsie-valley-grazing-corn)

## Spacing Requirements

**HIGH CONFIDENCE**

**Used:** 0.67 plants/sq ft (1 plant per 1.5 sq ft) - existing config

**Calculation verified:**
- In-row spacing: 8-12 inches (sources recommend thinning to this)
- Between-row spacing: 30-36 inches
- Area per plant: ~10" × 33" = 330 sq in = 2.3 sq ft → ~0.43 plants/sq ft
- OR: 12" × 30" = 360 sq in = 2.5 sq ft → 0.4 plants/sq ft

**Note:** Existing config (0.67 plants/sq ft = 1 plant per 1.5 sq ft) is **tighter** than typical OP corn recommendations. Sources suggest:
- [Lakeview Guide](https://lakevieworganicgrain.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/07/Growing-open-pollinated-corn-organically.pdf) - "thinned to 8-12" depending on irrigation"
- Lower population than hybrid corn recommended

**Existing spacing may be optimal for intensive garden use** but is tighter than field recommendations.

## Yield Per Plant

**MEDIUM CONFIDENCE**

**Used:** 0.25 lbs fresh weight per plant (existing config)

**Ear weight estimate:**
- Dent corn ears (7-8 inches) weigh approximately 0.5-1 lb fresh (husked)
- [General field corn reference](https://cookgeeks.net/how-much-does-corn-on-the-cob-weigh/) - "Field corn can weigh much more, often double [sweet corn]"
- Sweet corn medium ear: ~0.225 lbs
- Dent corn ear: ~0.5 lbs estimated

**Ears per plant:**
- Wapsie Valley typically produces 1-2 ears per plant (not explicitly stated in sources, standard for OP corn)

**Yield calculation:**
- If 1 ear per plant × 0.5 lbs = 0.5 lbs per plant
- If 0.5 ears per plant (some fail) × 0.5 lbs = 0.25 lbs per plant ✓

**Existing config (0.25 lbs) appears conservative** - may be accounting for:
- Lower success rate
- Second ear often smaller/not mature
- Or measuring partial ear yield

**University yield data:**
- [OPCORN](https://opcorn.com/product/wapsie-valley-seed-corn/) - "152 bushels per acre in university trials"
- At 18,000 plants/acre: 152 bu × 56 lbs/bu ÷ 18,000 = 0.47 lbs per plant (field scale)

**Recommendation:** Existing 0.25 lbs may be conservative for garden conditions. Could increase to 0.35-0.5 lbs if field data supports it.

**Existing config reasonable** - dent corn can be harvested over a longer period than sweet corn since it's not time-sensitive for eating quality.

## Seed Costs

**MEDIUM CONFIDENCE**

**Used:** $0.15 per plant (existing config)

**Pricing found:**
- [Green Haven](https://openpollinated.com/product/wapsie-valley-conventional-85-day-untreated/) - "$35-$129" depending on quantity
- [Green Haven Organic](https://openpollinated.com/product/wapsie-valley-organic-85-day-untreated/) - "$30-$109" depending on quantity
- Available in 1 lb, 10 lb, 50 lb units

**Seeds per pound calculation:**
- Dent corn: ~1,200-1,500 seeds per pound (larger kernels than sweet corn)
- Typical: ~1,400 seeds/lb

**Estimated cost per seed:**
- If $35 per 1 lb (1,400 seeds): $35 ÷ 1,400 = $0.025 per seed
- If $109 per 1 lb organic: $109 ÷ 1,400 = $0.078 per seed

**Existing config ($0.15) appears HIGH** - may be:
- Including planting losses
- Using retail organic pricing
- Or based on smaller packet pricing

**Recommendation:** Update to $0.03-$0.05 per plant for conventional seed, or document basis for $0.15.
