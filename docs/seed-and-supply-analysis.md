## Blocking Strategy: 2 Seeds Per Block

All soil-blocked crops get 2 seeds per block to maximize germination success.
- Single seed: 85% chance per block
- Two seeds: 1 - (0.15)^2 = **97.75% per block**
- Thin to strongest seedling after emergence

This doubles seed consumption but nearly eliminates empty blocks.
Seeds needed = (plants / 0.98) x 2.

Mix ratio: ~45% coir / 36% seed starter / 18% perlite.

### Block math

- 2" finished block: 2" x 2" x 2" = 8 in^3
- Compression ratio: ~2.5x (loose mix compressed into dense block)
- Loose mix per block: 8 x 2.5 = **20 in^3**
- 1 US quart = 57.75 in^3
- Total loose mix: 88 qt x 57.75 = 5,082 in^3
- **Theoretical max: 5,082 / 20 = ~254 blocks**
- **Practical yield (~90%, minor waste): ~230 blocks**
