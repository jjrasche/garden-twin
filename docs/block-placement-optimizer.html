Fit Quality

Fit Quality explained

Each crop has an optimal PPFD range where seedlings grow best (from published research).

Green = block PPFD is inside optimal range. Yellow = 10-25% outside range. Red = >25% outside — seedling will be leggy (too dim) or stressed (too bright).

The number on each block shows actual PPFD in Raw view, or +/- deviation from optimal in Fit view.
