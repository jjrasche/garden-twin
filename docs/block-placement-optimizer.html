Fit Quality explained

Two metrics: Range = is PPFD within acceptable range? Dev = how far from the optimum?

Tomato optimum: 240 (PMC study — best stem diameter). Kale optimum: 175 (DLI ~10 at 16h).

Green = in range, close to optimum. Yellow = marginal. Red = out of range or far from optimum.
